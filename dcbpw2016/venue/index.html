2016 DC - Baltimore Perl Workshop

Venue - Thumel Business Center, University of Baltimore

The Thumel Business Center of the University of Baltimore is very close to Penn Station, easily walkable. It is located near food and other attractions in the heart of Baltimore, MD.

11 W Mount Royal Ave Baltimore, MD, 21201 (Google Maps, Apple Maps)

Driving Directions / Parking:

Street parking is available near the venue, but as this is a busy section of the city, is not guaranteed. For this reason, and per the University of Baltimore's recommendation, we suggest the Fitzgerald Garage, located on the corner of Oliver St. and Mt. Royal Avenue (Google Maps, Apple Maps), which has the following rates:

  • $6 for up to 2 hours
  • $9 for up to 3 hours
  • $11 for up to 4 hours
  • $12 Daily Max

From Baltimore Beltway (I-695): Take Route 695 to exit 23, Jones Falls Expressway (I-83). Exit I-83 at the Maryland Avenue exit. From the exit ramp cross Maryland Avenue onto Oliver St. Make a right into the Fitzgerald Garage.

From Washington D.C.: Take I-495 North to I-95 North follow signs to downtown Baltimore. Make a right onto Pratt St. and then a left onto N. Charles St. Follow N. Charles St. and make a left onto Mt. Royal Avenue. Follow Mt. Royal Avenue, across Maryland Avenue, past the Lyric Theater, and make a right onto Oliver St. Make an immediate left in to the Fitzgerald Garage.

From Phil./N.Y.: Take I -95 South to I-695 West follow signs to Baltimore. Exit I-695 at the Jones Falls Expressway; exit 23(I-83). Exit I-83 at the Maryland Avenue exit. Go straight on to Oliver Street and make an immediate right into the Fitzgerald Garage.

For more detailed directions, please consult the University of Baltimore's website.

Access via Public Transportation:

Note to DC attendees: The WMATA's SmarTrip® is fully cross-compatible with Baltimore's CharmCard®, and can be used to pay for for MTA, Light Rail, and Subway travel.

  • MTA Buses: there are many in the area, but five routes have stops within one block of the Thumel Business Center.
    • Route 3 (Northbound)
    • Route 11 (Northbound and Southbound)
    • Route 21 (Northbound and Southbound)
    • Route 61 (Northbound)
    • Route 64 (Northbound)
  • Subway: The Maryland State Office Building Metro Station is 4 blocks from the University.
    • Google Maps Directions
    • Apple Maps Directions
  • Light Rail: The University of Baltimore/Mount Royal Station, with service from BWI Airport, is located adjacent to the Fitzgerald Garage.
    • Google Maps Directions
    • Apple Maps Directions
  • Charm City Circulator: The Purple Route of Baltimore's free bus service has two stops within blocks of the University.
    • 310 - Penn Station Northbound (Apple Maps Directions, Google Maps Directions)
    • 321 - Penn Station Southbound (Apple Maps Directions, Google Maps Directions)
  • Intercity Train: Baltimore's Penn Station is just around the corner from the Thumel Business Center.
    • Amtrak has lines going through Baltimore from many US Cities.
    • MARC trains provide an affordable commuter route between DC and Baltimore.
